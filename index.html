Learn More About Us

Cutting Edge is a well-qualified lawn and landscaping company that has been in business here in Brevard County for 20 plus years. Our focus has always been on providing our clients with excellent customer service, reliability and of course attention to detailed and quality work.

Our crews are well trained and professional. Whether it is lawn maintenance, landscaping or enhancements our team is professional and efficient. We look forward to developing new relationships and growing our company with the same integrity that our clients have come to depend on over the years. Â 

At Cutting Edge, we pride ourselves on staying at the forefront of landscaping technology and sustainable practices. Our team regularly undergoes training to learn about the latest industry innovations, environmentally friendly techniques, and region-specific plant care. This dedication to continuous improvement allows us to offer our clients not just maintenance, but also expert advice on how to enhance and preserve their outdoor spaces for years to come. From water-efficient irrigation systems to native plant selections that thrive in Brevard County's climate, we're committed to creating beautiful, sustainable landscapes that our clients and the environment can enjoy.
